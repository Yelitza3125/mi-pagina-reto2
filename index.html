the history

The island’s beauty has been waxed lyrical through the years, absolutely incomes the epithet “kalliste” (the most beautiful). Together with “strogili”, this turned into one of the former names lent to the island over its afflicted history. Its records are certainly one of the battles and conquer, of a sad eruption and a blossoming renaissance – events that haven’t altered Santorini’s attraction however that undoubtedly scarred it.
